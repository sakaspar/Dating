# Privacy Policy — Doukhou

**Last updated:** March 31, 2026

## 1. Information We Collect

- **Account Info:** Email, password (hashed), name, age, gender
- **Profile Data:** Bio, photos, relationship intentions, activity preferences
- **Location:** GPS coordinates for nearby match discovery (50km radius)
- **Messages:** Chat content between matched users (encrypted in transit)
- **Usage Data:** App interactions, swipe patterns (for improving matches)

## 2. How We Use Your Data

- To match you with compatible users based on shared activities
- To enable messaging and date proposals between matched users
- To improve our matching algorithm
- To ensure safety (report/block features)

## 3. Data Storage

- All data is stored on secure servers
- Messages are stored for the duration of your account
- Deleted accounts have data removed within 30 days
- We do NOT sell your data to third parties

## 4. Location Data

- Your location is used only to find nearby matches
- Precise coordinates are never shared with other users
- Users see approximate distance only (e.g., "3km away")
- You can disable location sharing at any time in settings

## 5. Photos

- Uploaded photos are stored securely
- Photos are visible to potential matches and current matches only
- You can delete any photo at any time
- Photos are compressed for efficient delivery

## 6. Safety

- You can block any user at any time
- Reports are reviewed and appropriate action is taken
- Blocked users cannot see your profile or contact you

## 7. Your Rights

- Access your data: Available in your profile settings
- Delete your account: Available in settings → account
- Data export: Contact us for a copy of your data
- Withdraw consent: Delete your account

## 8. Children's Privacy

Doukhou is for users 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

## 9. Contact

For privacy concerns: support@doukhou.com

---

This privacy policy applies to the Doukhou mobile application and all associated services.
